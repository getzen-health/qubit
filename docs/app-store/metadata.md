## Description (4000 chars max)
KQuarks transforms your Apple Health data into actionable intelligence. Connecting seamlessly to the Health app, KQuarks pulls in data from your iPhone, Apple Watch, and connected devices, then surfaces the patterns, trends, and anomalies that matter most to your wellbeing.

**Deep Analytics Across 200+ Metrics**
Most health apps show you a number. KQuarks shows you what that number means. Explore heart rate variability trends across months, compare sleep stage distributions week over week, track your aerobic fitness trajectory with VO2 Max history, and understand how your training load relates to your recovery — all in one place. Every metric comes with context: historical baselines, peer comparisons, and trend lines so you always know whether you're moving in the right direction.

**AI Morning Briefings**
Start every day with a personalised health summary delivered straight to your notification shade. KQuarks' AI analyses the previous night's sleep, yesterday's activity, your HRV, resting heart rate, and dozens of other signals, then synthesises them into a plain-English briefing that tells you how recovered you are and what to focus on today. No dashboards to open, no numbers to interpret — just a clear read on your body before your feet hit the floor.

**Anomaly Detection**
KQuarks watches for deviations from your personal baseline around the clock. Unusually elevated resting heart rate? Significant HRV drop? Sleep efficiency falling off a cliff? You'll get an alert the moment something looks out of the ordinary, giving you the chance to rest, investigate, or check in with a doctor before a small signal becomes a real problem.

**Built for Athletes, Biohackers, and the Health-Curious**
Whether you're training for an ultra-marathon, experimenting with sleep optimisation, managing a chronic condition, or simply curious about what your body is telling you, KQuarks meets you where you are. Sport-specific views cover running biomechanics, cycling power zones, swimming stroke analysis, and more. Body composition trends, menstrual cycle insights, glucose pattern visualisation, and longevity metrics round out a feature set that grows with your interests.

**Your Data Stays Yours**
KQuarks never sells your health data. Raw HealthKit data is read directly on-device and synced to your private, encrypted Supabase database — not a shared data lake. You can delete your account and every byte of stored data at any time from within the app. AI features run through Anthropic's Claude API under strict data-processing agreements; no health data is used to train AI models.

**Get Started in Under a Minute**
Sign in, grant Health permissions, and KQuarks begins populating your history immediately. No manual entry, no wearable pairing rituals, no subscription required to see your own data.

Download KQuarks today and finally understand what your health data has been trying to tell you.
